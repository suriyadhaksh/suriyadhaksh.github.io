Hello, I'm Suriya.

I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at Iowa State University. I work on computational modeling of multiphysics systems for applications in micro-dialysis chips, large-scale desalinators, droplet transfer, and electromagnetic casting. I also build scalable and parallelizable finite element solvers for coupled PDEs governing fluid flow, electrokinetics, heat transfer, and material thermodynamics.

I also enjoy the analysis part of my work, including error and stability analysis, asymptotic approximations and high-performance scaling studies.
