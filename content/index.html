Hello, I'm Suriya.

I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at Iowa State University. My research lies at the intersection of mathematical modeling, numerical methods, and high-performance computing for multiphysics simulations. I study electrochemical transport, interfacial dynamics, and multiscale phenomena, along with the development of robust algorithms and scalable simulation tools.

I also enjoy the analysis part of my work, including error and stability analysis, asymptotic approximations and performance scaling studies.
